A Dust SEO practitioner describes shifting from a fully manual SEO process (keyword research, SERP analysis, content briefs, reporting, internal linking, competitor checks) to one driven by AI agents. The article walks through four SEO agents the author uses day-to-day, starting with one for finding new keyword opportunities, while keeping human judgment for strategy and positioning.
