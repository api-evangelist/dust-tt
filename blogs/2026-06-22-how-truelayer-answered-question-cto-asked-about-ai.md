At fast-growing fintechs, engineering and product teams already using Claude or ChatGPT often ask their CTO why the company should pay for a dedicated AI platform instead. This piece looks at how TrueLayer, one of Europe's leading open banking platforms, and its CTO and co-founder Luca Martinetti answered that question by thinking about AI at the organizational level rather than the individual one.
